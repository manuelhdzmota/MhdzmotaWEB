DESDIBUJAR

"Desdibujar" is a photographic project that explores and celebrates diversity. Unfortunately, in 2019, Mexico ranked second in hate crimes against the LGBTTTI+ community, with seven out of ten people experiencing some form of violence. This is why "Desdibujar" portrays diversity. The purpose: to reach a world where being yourself is not a crime, a world where no one dies because of who they are or who they love.
