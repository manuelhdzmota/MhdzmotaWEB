About me

Manuel H. Mota, photographer, filmmaker and digital artist currently based in Mexico city. Inspired by human beauty and the mundane, fashion, sexuality, queerness, magic realism and the bizarre, he uses his art as an exploration of identity and his meaning in this world, and as an excuse to document and celebrate the beauty the world has to offer.


He looks up to artists such as Nan Goldin, Guy Bourdin, Robert Mapplethorpe, David LaChapelle, Jared French, among others.

He seeks to create meaningful projects, both aesthetically pleasing and with a trascendental message.
