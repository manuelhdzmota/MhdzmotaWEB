Manuel H. Mota

Catalog

Photographer, filmmaker and digital artist currently based in Mexico city. Inspired by human beauty and the mundane, fashion, sexuality, queerness, magic realism and the bizarre, he uses his art as an exploration of identity and his meaning in this world.


He looks up to artists such as Nan Goldin, Guy Bourdin, Robert Mapplethorpe, Jared French, among others. He seeks to create meaningful projects, both aesthetically pleasing and with a trascendental message.
